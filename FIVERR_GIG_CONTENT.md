## ❓ FAQ (Add these to your gig)

### Q: Do I need a Shopify account before ordering?
**A:** No! I'll guide you through creating your Shopify account, or I can set it up for you. Shopify offers a free trial so you can get started without paying upfront.

### Q: Do you provide the theme?
**A:** Yes! I use high-converting premium-quality free themes. If you want a specific paid theme, you'll need to purchase it, or I can recommend the best one for your niche.

### Q: Can you build a dropshipping store?
**A:** Absolutely! I specialize in dropshipping stores and can set up DSers, Spocket, CJ Dropshipping, or any sourcing app you prefer. Products will be imported and ready to sell.

### Q: Will my store be mobile friendly?
**A:** Yes, 100%. Over 70% of shoppers buy on mobile, so every store I build is fully responsive and looks perfect on phones, tablets, and desktops.

### Q: Do you write product descriptions?
**A:** I add products with professional descriptions. If you provide your own content, I'll use that. For the Premium package, I optimize all descriptions for SEO.

### Q: Can you connect my custom domain?
**A:** Yes! Domain connection is included in the Premium package. For other packages, I can add it as an extra. I'll also set up your email forwarding.

### Q: Will you teach me how to manage the store?
**A:** Yes! I provide a walkthrough so you'll know how to add products, fulfill orders, manage inventory, and handle everything yourself.

### Q: Do you offer support after delivery?
**A:** Yes, I offer after-delivery support to make sure everything runs smoothly. If you need any changes or have questions, just reach out.

6. **Do you have a domain name?** (If yes, share it. If no, I can help you choose one)

7. **What payment methods do you want?** (Stripe, PayPal, Shop Pay, etc.)

8. **Any specific apps or features you need?** (Reviews, email marketing, upsells, etc.)

9. **Where do you ship to?** (Domestic only, worldwide, specific countries)

10. **Anything else I should know?** (Special requests, timeline, etc.)

1. **Price competitively** — Start at $80-$100 for your first few orders to get reviews
